How It Works

Decentrifi provides powerful analytics for your smart contracts, giving you insights that were previously impossible to obtain.

1
🏷️

Plugin Your Contract Address

Simply enter your smart contract address, and Decentrifi immediately begins capturing and analyzing all transactions interacting with your contract.

  • Instant deployment tracking
  • Support for any EVM chain
  • Works with custom ABIs automatically
2
🔍

Deep Transaction Analysis

Using trace_filter technology, we capture both direct and internal transactions, providing unprecedented insight into how your contract is being used.

  • Function call tracking
  • Gas optimization metrics
  • Error pattern detection

Real-Time Insights Dashboard

Access beautiful visualizations and analytics that help you understand user behavior, optimize performance, and make data-driven decisions.

  • Transfer volume tracking
  • Custom time-range reports

Continuous Monitoring

Decentrifi runs 24/7, automatically updating your analytics and providing real-time alerts when important events occur on your contract.

  • Historical data retention
